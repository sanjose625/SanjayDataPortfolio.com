About Me

Hi there! I'm Sanjay Pushparajan, a passionate data analyst with a strong affinity for turning raw data into actionable insights. With a Power BI certification and proficiency in SQL, Pandas, NumPy, and Power Query, I'm equipped to dive into complex datasets and extract valuable information. I hold a BSc degree in Computer Science and possess hands-on experience in curating and preparing diverse datasets for analysis.

My Journey

My journey in the realm of data analysis began with my role as a diligent data annotator at i3systems.ai from June 2022 to June 2023. During this time, I worked with integrated digital workspaces like Accops and Power AI, contributing to the efficient access and annotation of data. My responsibilities included labeling and annotating data to fuel the development of machine learning and AI models. I maintained an unwavering commitment to accuracy and quality, ensuring that the labeled data was reliable for training models.

Internships and Growth

These opportunities have fortified my analytical acumen and expanded my proficiency in handling diverse datasets.
